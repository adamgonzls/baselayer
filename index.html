Deployment Scenario

BASELAYER CORE units are configurable in many deployment scenarios. This deployment delivers up to 1.5MW of Critical IT power and Cooling for up to one hundred fifty (150) 50U racks @ N redundancy. BASELAYER deployments can consist of both power and data (IT) modules configured and tested from the factory with an enhanced integrated Module Control Unit (MCU) providing access to real-time module sensors. Power and cooling redundancy thresholds, along with analytics and historic trending, are all available through BASELAYER RunSmart OS
